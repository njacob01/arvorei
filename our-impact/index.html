Our Impact

While supporting our clients to make a greater impact in the world by removing language barriers, we’re making a difference of our own!

Cameras for Girls

Many young women in Uganda face the harsh realities of gender inequality and poverty. Despite their talent and aspirations to become photographers or journalists, societal barriers and a male-dominated field often make their dreams feel unattainable.

The opportunity to own a camera is transformative—it unlocks creativity, independence, and opportunity. With mentorship and training, these young women gain the skills to tell compelling stories and build paths toward meaningful employment.

$10/month – Equips 2 women with support to cover their WIFI data and transportation, ensuring safe travel back and forth to the editing lab and access to essential online training and Zoom sessions to help her learn and expand her skills.

$25/month – Enables 4 women to receive professionally designed LinkedIn profiles, build global networks, and craft polished resumes, empowering them to thrive in the competitive job market.

$50/month – Helps purchase 1 camera (Canon G1x, a camera bag, SD card, extra battery ) kit for 1 woman in our program.
$100/month or $1,200/year – Will pay for a leadership course for 2 women via our partnership with Phenomenal Women Global.

$125/month – Sponsors 1 girl with a camera kit, year-long training, vital data, transportation, and a professionally designed LinkedIn profile, resume, and 6-month mentorship to help her be job-ready by the end of the program.

Please join us in supporting their Frame her Future Campaign and help change lives, one girl at a time!

Brands for Better Foundation

Brands for Better is a volunteer-driven not-for-profit based in Vancouver that is creating opportunities for impact-led brands and individuals to collaborate and take action on local issues to make the city a better place to live.

The organization believes that the biggest and most impactful change is created when brands unite their power, resources, ideas, strategies, experience, and their people. They connect brands and individuals who want to create positive change with opportunities to tackle pressing environmental and social issues in our shared community.

As a member of their Executive Team and Funding Lead, Kim is leveraging her many years of not-for-profit fundraising, grant research & writing, and communications experience to attract new sources of funding to ensure Brands for Better can continue to make an impact locally and look to expand beyond Vancouver.

If you live in or visit Greater Vancouver, be sure to check out the calendar of events below and come check out this force for good!

Treehouse Collective

Launched in May 2022 by Arvorei founder Kim Jacob, the Treehouse Collective brings together freelance translators across the globe who meet on a monthly basis to network and learn with their peers. They grow their businesses, learn new ways to achieve well-being, and combat the isolation and loneliness that often accompany the career of a translator.

The Treehouse Collective is free to join and offers members:

  • Monthly business workshops & networking sessions
  • Workshops by wellness experts
  • WhatsApp group
  • Shared resources & meeting recordings on Google Drive
  • Opportunity to promote services for translators

If you’re a professional translator and would like more information about joining the Treehouse Collective, reach out to Kim Jacob here.
